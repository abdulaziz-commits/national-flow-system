Core Flow Logic

Flows move across assets within the environment, enabled by the Flow Operating System.

Material Flow | التدفق المادي

Moves physical inputs, goods, and outputs across productive and logistic assets.

  • Moves through: factories, roads, ports, supply chains
  • Main risk: transport bottlenecks
  • SF reading: compares physical capacity vs physical demand

Energy Flow | التدفق الطاقي

Moves power, fuel, and usable energy across national systems.

  • Moves through: grids, fuel systems, energy networks
  • Main risk: interruption of supply
  • SF reading: energy availability vs system demand

Human Flow | التدفق البشري

Represents the movement of people, labor, skills, and operating capacity.

  • Moves through: institutions, workplaces, mobility systems
  • Main risk: decision delay, skills mismatch, access limits
  • SF reading: human capacity vs operational demand

Knowledge Flow | التدفق المعرفي

Moves data, insight, expertise, instructions, and innovation across the system.

  • Moves through: digital systems, governance channels, learning systems
  • Main risk: fragmentation, delay, weak transfer
  • SF reading: knowledge availability vs decision and execution demand

Financial Flow | التدفق المالي

Moves capital, funding, liquidity, and financial allocations across all assets and programs.

  • Moves through: budgets, banks, allocation systems, investment channels
  • Main risk: allocation pressure, delayed liquidity, imbalance in funding
  • SF reading: financial capacity vs financial demand

Plan Logic

The five-year plan is executed through programs, measured by flow efficiency (SF), and aligned with Oman Vision 2040 layers.

Program Performance

Program Vision Layer Primary Flow Capacity Demand SF Status Action
Program A Economy Material 120 100 1.20 Strong Expand
Program B Governance Human 70 100 0.70 Bottleneck Immediate Action
Program C People & Society Knowledge 100 100 1.00 Critical Monitor
Program D Environment Financial 110 90 1.22 Stable Sustain

Plan-to-Vision Alignment

  • Environment → resource stability and sustainability
  • Governance → decision speed and execution discipline
  • People & Society → operating capacity and human readiness
  • Economy → production, value creation, and competitiveness

Decision Signals

  • SF > 1 → Expand or sustain capacity
  • SF = 1 → Monitor and protect balance
  • SF < 1 → Immediate intervention required

Strategic Note

Plans move the system, but flow efficiency determines whether execution is stable, critical, or bottlenecked.
